About Us

Waootrips is a trusted Travel company based in Gurgaon (India), has been a specialist in organising various types of customised tour packages of India.We give travelers innovative tools that keep them safe and productive, and help them make good choices on the road.In short, we help our clients travel smart and achieve more. With 14+ years of industry experience,We offer Handcrafted trips and experiences for perfect family holidays,exotic honeymoons,fun college trips and even exciting corporate trips, based on clients need, interest & budget with promising services in mind. Mission: Our Mission is to perform and deliver excellent quality service to our clients. Providing them unforgettable travel, enjoyable, with guaranteed services that exceed their expectations With this objective, we aim to earn the reputation as "Your Preferred Travel Partner." Vision: To become the leading niche travel company in India' providing specialized vacations to meet any clientele need around the globe.
